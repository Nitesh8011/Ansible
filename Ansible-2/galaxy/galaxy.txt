To import role from ansible galaxy
ansible-galaxy role install <role_name>

roles will be saved in ~/.ansible/roles

# How to use imported roles
make one role book and mention that imported role - check galaxy.yml

Then Run:
ansible-playbook -i /home/nitesh/Documents/Documents_personal/ansible/inventory.ini galaxy.yml


Push Role in Ansible-galaxy
ansible-galaxy import <git_username> <repo_name> --token <token>
